About UB-Apartments

A Better Choice Than Apartment Complexes or Dormitory Style Living

UB-Apartments offers classic Buffalo architecture in spacious one, two, and three bedroom apartments — affordably priced and thoughtfully maintained for student life. We specialize in roommate pairing and individual leases, making it easy to find your perfect living situation.

Our apartments feature in-house free laundry, fully furnished options, and a secure, student-friendly environment. We are conveniently located near the UB Stampede Bus route, giving you easy access to all University at Buffalo campuses and downtown Buffalo.
